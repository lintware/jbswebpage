Project Applications

Our welders join metal parts using various methods like arc, MIG, or TIG welding. They read drawings and prepare materials for welding work.

They perform hot work safely under proper permits and supervision, check weld quality, and may assist with NDT testing.

All welders follow strict safety procedures and wear full protective gear during work.

Experience Highlights

Technical Skills

Fitters install, assemble, and align pipes, valves, and mechanical equipment. They cut, fit, and weld piping sections and replace gaskets and seals.

They assist with equipment dismantling, reinstallation, and pipe support setup while following technical drawings.

Setting up safety barriers, moving tools and equipment, and supporting daily tasks are part of their responsibilities.
