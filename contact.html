Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and processes

We handle a wide range of industrial projects including daily plant maintenance, annual shutdowns (SDM), piping fabrication and installation, electrical & instrumentation work, insulation services, tankage cleaning, and comprehensive Manpower Support for major industrial facilities.

We can mobilize our team within 24-48 hours for emergency situations. Our 24/7 availability ensures that urgent maintenance and emergency shutdown support can be provided immediately when needed.

We maintain bizSAFE STAR certification, ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, ISO 45001:2018, and ASME Section IX welding certifications. Our procedures are approved under Det-Norske Veritas (DNV) class for maximum quality assurance.

Yes, we provide both short-term and long-term manpower solutions. Our skilled workforce includes certified welders, mechanical fitters, supervisors, safety coordinators, and specialized trade workers for projects ranging from a few days to several years.

We ensure quality through our ISO-certified quality management system, certified welding procedures (WPS/PQR), experienced supervisors, comprehensive documentation, NDT testing support, and regular quality audits throughout the project lifecycle.
